    The Media's Obsession With The O.J. Simpson Trial

    If you asked most people who were alive during the mid-'90s, they would have vivid memories of O.J. Simpson in court. Often labeled the trial of the century, the media spent weeks obsessing over every detail of O.J.'s trial.

    As a result of all the attention paid to that trial, a lot of people know where they were when O.J. was found not guilty. Furthermore, the media paid a lot of attention when O.J. was charged with a different crime, when he went to prison, and when he got out.

    Even people who weren't alive during the '90s likely feel like they have a good idea of what happened as a result of shocking documentaries and The People vs. O.J. Simpson.

    While millions of people have been fascinated by the events surrounding O.J.'s trial and what has taken place since then, it seems pretty clear that at least one person feels very differently. After all, according to reports, O.J. and Nicole Brown Simpson's daughter Sydney has done everything she can to avoid the spotlight focused on the trial.

    What Happened To O.J. Simpson And Nicole Brown Simpson's Daughter?

    No longer the child who was captured in photos alongside her parents before her mother's tragic passing, Sydney Simpson has become an adult.

    What's The Truth About Sydney Simpson's Name?

    In an ideal world, nobody would bug Sydney Simpson since she has made it clear she wants to have a life that is as normal as possible. Sadly, however, even though Sydney went to great lengths to evade the press, she wasn't always successful.

    Related: O.J Simpson Trolled After Claiming He's Scared To Bump Into Wife's 'Real Killer'

    In 2014, Georgia Newsday revealed how Sydney's life had changed at that time. According to that report, Sydney was working at an Atlanta restaurant as a server without her co-workers or customers at that time knowing who she really was.

    The reason Sydney was able to remain anonymous until that report was that her co-workers reportedly had been told her name was Portia.

    While Georgia Newsday's report revealed that Sydney had adopted a different name, there wasn't any indication that she changed her name legally. Since then, there haven't been any other reports of Sydney going by anything other than her birth name.

    Since Sydney doesn't talk to the press, there is no way to know for sure what has gone on with her name. However, since recent reports about her life refer to O.J. and Nicole's daughter by her birth name, it certainly seems like her legal name is Sydney as of this writing.
