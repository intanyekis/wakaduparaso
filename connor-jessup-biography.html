    Who is Connor Jessup?

    Connor Jessup is a Canadian actor, director, and writer well-known as the role of Ben Mason on the TNT Science fiction TV series Falling Skies. 

    Jessup won the 2016 FilmOut Programming Award in Outstanding Emerging Talent for Closet Monster.

    In 2020, he is playing the role of Tyler Locke in the TV series Locke & Key.

    Connor Jessup- Early Life, Family

    Connor Jessup was born as Connor William Jessup on 23rd June 1994 in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. He was born to mother Brenda Thurston. He has not revealed his father’s name.

    Similarly, he has a brother Liam with whom he was raised. He has not revealed his education details.

    Connor Jessup- Professional career

    Acting

    Connor Jessup started acting at the age of 11. His roles include in TV series like The Jon Dore Television Show, The Saddle Club, King, Falling Skies: The Enemy Within, Falling Skies, American Crime, and Locke & Key.

    Similarly, his movie roles include in Blackbird, Skating to New York, Closet Monster, Strange But True, Clifton Hill, and White Lie.

    His short movie appearance is in What Doesn’t Kill You, Fragments, Crazy House, and others.

    Filmmaker

    As a director, Connor Jessup has worked in Night Flight, Casey MQ: The Constant, Lira’s Forest, Boy, Little Coffins and I Don’t Hurt Anymore!

    As a producer and writer, he worked in Lira’s Forest, Boy, Little Coffins, Amy George, and I Don’t Hurt Anymore.
