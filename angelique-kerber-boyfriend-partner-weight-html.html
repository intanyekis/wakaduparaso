    Bremen-native, Angelique Kerber, is a German professional tennis player and former world No. 1. She is also a three-time Grand Slam champion. Kerber rose to fame upon reaching the semifinals of the 2011 US Open as the No. 92 ranked player in the world.  

    Kerber grew up in Kiel and started playing tennis at the age of three, eventually joining the junior circuit. She currently resides in Puszczykowo, Poland.

    Wimbledon 2018: Serena Williams faces off Angelique Kerber in the finals (Published on 14 July 2018 )

    Angelique Kerber’s Bio: Height, Weight, Ethnicity, Body Measurements

    Kerber was born  to parents, Slawomir and Beata Kerber, on 16 January 1988 in Bremen, Germany. She also has a sister named Jessica. 

    The pro tennis player stands tall with a height of 5' 8" (1.73 M). She is a Capricorn and weighs around 73 kg or 161 pounds. Her Ethnicity is Polish.

    Her body measurements of 37-26-35 inches (94-66-89 cm) perfectly complements her height and persona. 

    Angelique Kerber Became #1 At Age 28

    Her 2016 tennis season officially began on January 5, 2016, with the start of the 2016 Brisbane International. She entered the season as the number 10 ranked player and the defending champion at four tournaments.

    She won several trophies in 2016 including the Australian Open, the Stuttgart Open, the US Open, and more. Her accumulated earnings that year was over $18 million.

    Having made her professional debut in 2003, she ascended to the top of the rankings on September 12, 2016.

    Angelique Kerber’s Net Worth

    Angelique Kerber relishes her massive net worth of $30 million. This comes as no surprise since she is one of the best professional tennis players in the world, who is competing toe-to-toe with tennis legend, Serena Williams.

    Aside from that, she earns additional revenue from her endorsements and contracts with Adidas, Arena 11, Tennis Flex, and Yonex. She is also the ambassador for Rolex.

    Furthermore, Kerber was the second-highest-paid athlete in the world in 2017, with earnings of $12.6 million, including $7.6 million in prize money.

    Angelique Kerber and Torben Beltz

    On 17 November 2017, Angelique announced her split with her coach Torben Beltz, after working with him for a total of eight years in her career.

    The partnership which flourished with two Major titles and the number one ranking in 2016, struggled throughout 2017. Kerber fell out of the top 20 after an unbelievable route.

    It came as no surprise when sher announced that she was parting ways with Torben Beltz.

    In 2020, Angelique Kerber rehired Torben Beltz after she parted ways with her coach at the time, Dieter Kindlmann.

    At the 2021 US Open, Kerber lost at the round of 16 to 18 year old Leylah Fernandez. After the tournament, however, Kerber had nothing but praises for her young 18 year old opponent.

    Angelique Kerber’s Love Life

    Kerber prefers to keep her love life under wraps. Not much is known about her romantic relationships either. So, for those asking questions like "who is Angelique Kerber's husband?" "Is Angelique Kerber married?" "Who is Angelique Kerber's boyfirend?" "Who is Angelique Kerber's partner?" She made the answer pretty clear in 2018.

    In July 2018, she revealed that she wasn't looking for a boyfriend, citing that she wanted to focus on her Tennis career instead. When pressed about the topic in an interview with Tennisworldusa, she said, 

    That's the question I was waiting for. I am not in a rush. If you do not expect it, he comes, it's like this. I will figure it out.

    It had long been speculated that the beautiful and athletic woman was dating her professional partner, Torben Beltz. But after their split in 2017, that rumor was put to rest.

    Although she has declared that she isn't in a rush, she hasn't closed the door on her love life. Maybe just like she said, her soul-mate will come into her life when least expected.
