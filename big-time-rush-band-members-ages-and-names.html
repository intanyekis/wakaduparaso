    Big Time Rush Band Members Ages

    Big Time Rush Band members ages are either 32 or 33. Two members, Kendall and James are 32 whereas Logan and Carlos are 33 years old.

    Kendall Schmidt

    Big Time Rush singer Kendall Schmidt is the founder of the pop boy band. He also serves as an actor, dancer, music producer, and songwriter.

    Schmidt has starred on television shows including Without a Trace, Gilmore Girls, School of Rock, ER, Phil of the Future, Ghost Whisperer, and Frasier.

    Born in Wichita, Kansas, Kendall is a married man. The 32-year-old officially exchanged the wedding vows with Mica Von Turkovich, his girlfriend of seven years, on June 30, 2022.

    James Maslow 

    One of the four members of Big Time Rush, James Maslow, has also flourished as an actor besides his triumphant legacy in the music industry. 

    He is known for starring as Kevin Mohr on the series Sequestered. Maslow also secured the fourth position on the 18th season of Dancing with the Stars in 2014.

    Is James Maslow married?

    No, the singer isn’t married but is in a relationship with his girlfriend Caitlin Spears. The couple celebrated their 4th anniversary on April 11, 2023.

    Logan Henderson

    Likewise, member Logan also has a successful career as an actor in the film industry. He starred as Teenage Boy #2 on the TV program Friday Night Lights.

    Henderson launched his solo career following the hiatus of the band in 2014. He dropped a debut solo single titled Sleepwalker on January 27, 2017. 

    Correspondingly, the singer released his second and third singles ‘Bite My Tongue’ and ‘Speak of the Devil’ on September 15, 2017, and October 30, 2017, respectively.

    Logan Henderson girlfriend Jasmin is open and vocal about their relationship in public. As of now, Logan is in a relationship with Jasmin since 2019. 

    Carlos PenaVega 

    Big Time Rush band member Carlos was born on August 15, 1989, in Columbia, Missouri, United States. 

    Carlos PenaVega wife Alexa Vega, an actress, crossed paths with him at a bible study in 2012. In fact, she made a guest appearance starring as Carlos’ onscreen girlfriend in the series Big Time Rush in July 2013.

    Later, the onscreen couple made things official and engaged in August 2013. A few months later, they tied the knot on January 4, 2014, in Mexico.

    Carlos and Alexa are the parents of three children. two sons and a daughter. The couple boasts a YouTube channel named La Vida PenaVega, currently with over 810K subscribers.

    Is Big Time Rush Still A Band?

    Yes, Big Time Rush is still a band. They were on an indefinite hiatus that lasted from 2014 until when they resumed their activities in 2021.

    The four-membered band halted their activities in 2013 to provide some quality time to work on their own music. In between the time of their hiatus, Kendall and Dustin Belt released music as a duo.

    Carlos, also an actor, spent his time in Hallmark movies, and reality shows like Dancing with the Stars. Not to forget, he also happily exchanged wedding vows with his wife Alexa PenaVega in 2014.

    Likewise, James continued his already triumphant acting career. In fact, like Carlos, he also had been on ‘Dancing with the Stars.’

    In the meantime, Logan launched his solo career with the release of a debut solo album in 2018. 

    Is Big Time Rush Releasing A New Album 2023?

    Big Time Rush announced a new album release and a new single in 2023. They confirmed the news prior to heading to their 2023 summer tour.

    Following a decade-long hiatus, the pop band announced to release of the new album on June 9, 2023. The boy band pronounced that their tour early on February only after they finished their ‘Nostalgia’ run.

    BTR dropped a new single ‘Can’t Get Enough’ on the day they released their upcoming 2023 tour ‘Can’t Get Enough Tour’ which is set to start off on June 22 from Fort Worth, Texas.

    Even since the announcement, the pop band’s genuine fans have been going crazy for their highly anticipated album. If any one is interested in their tour, they can grab tickets from Ticketmaster. 

    Big Time Rush And One Direction

    Big Time Rush and One Direction were tourmates in 2012. One Direction opened the tour for the pop band as an in-house record label mates.

    Kendall unveiled that they weren’t happy with the fact that One Direction was opening for them. He stated that there were 2000 girls outside their show jamming to their songs although they were headlining the tour.

    The founder of the band, Schmidt, also stated that they felt like being used by their record label to promote One Direction.  He also added that the only prime reason the two boy bands were together on a tour was that they were from the same record label.

    Is Big Time Rush A Real Band?

    Yes, Big Time Rush is a real band. Four of them were brought together through auditions by producers like many other bands.

    Ever since the creation of their band, all four of them have been working hard on forging their names in the music scene even after more than a decade after its inception.

    The boy band was signed to Nick Records in 2009 and later transferred to Columbia Records. As a group, all four boys starred in the TV series, Big Time Rush, which ran from November 28, 2009, to July 25, 2013.

    For the series, the band released their eponymous debut single on November 29, 2009. The show also saw the releases of the band’s promotional singles including ‘Any Kind of Guy’ and ‘City is Ours.’
