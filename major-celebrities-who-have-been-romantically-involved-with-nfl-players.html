    Although many Hollywood stars often end up with fellow A-listers, many also mesh very well with NFL players. With their busy schedules and regular travel plans, professional athletes and celebrities have careers that are similar in one too many ways. And, it's actually fairly common for female celebrities to mingle with the men from America's most-watched sport. Lest we not forget that NFL players are celebrities in their very own right, too. With their lavish lifestyles, picturesque mansions, and expensive cars, their lives are just as glamorous as those of Hollywood actors, singers, and models.

    8 Jessica Simpson And Eric Johnson

    Breaking News: Singer Jessica Simpson did move on from Nick Lachey.

    The songstress, who was in the tabloids way back when for her highly-profiled relationship with Nick Lachey found everlasting love with a professional athlete. Eric Johnson, a former tight end for the San Francisco 49ers is the lucky man married to the stunning songbird that once graced our screens in "Daisy Dukes."

    They tied the knot in 2014 and their obsession with one another after eleven years hasn't faded. They also share their happiness with three bundles of joy!

    7 Kerry Washington And Nnamdi Asomugha

    Kerry Washington definitely tunes into her emotions when she's acting, however, in real life, she's much more guarded.

Washington married the former NFL cornerback, who was on the roster for the Oakland Raiders, Philadelphia Eagles, and San Fransisco 49ers in an intimate and private Idaho ceremony.

    5 Kendra Wilkinson And Hank Baskett

    The Girls Next Door alum is now a single lady following her split with the father of her children, Hank Baskett.

    The former Playboy playmate is now focusing on the "mom stuff," since her divorce from Baskett, which was finalized in 2018. The duo were a force to be reckoned with for nine years, but are now doing the whole coparenting thing. According to Us Weekly, Wilkinson spoke of her split from the former NFL wide receiver saying, "It is stable. We are [in a better place]. Time heals all."

    4 Ciara And Russell Wilson

    Talk about #couplegoals, this songstress and her athletic man have been going strong for years!

    The Seattle Seahawks quarterback and his wifey make a superstar couple. The couple married in a secret wedding in London in 2016 and have been living happily ever after since - now accompanied by their three adorable children.
