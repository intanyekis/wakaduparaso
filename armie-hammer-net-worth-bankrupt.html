    He was Oscar-nominated for his performance as Oliver in Call Me by Your Name. But after a series of allegations, Armie Hammer is now reportedly broke.

    Armie Hammer Was Pictured Allegedly Working At A Hotel

    TMZ shared photos of the former Gossip Girl star that were taken in June. The pictures allegedly show Hammer seated at a desk at a resort, wearing a polo shirt and holding a pen. The pictures appear to show him speaking to individuals seated across from him. At first there were rumors Hammer was a hotel concierge. A flyer making the rounds on social media depicting Hammer, 35, as a concierge at Morritt's Resort in Grand Cayman. It later emerged the father of two was allegedly selling timeshares.

    In an email to PEOPLE, Hammer's attorney Andrew Brettler said, "I don't know anything about this." He also said in a statement to Entertainment Weekly, "I can't confirm or deny it because Armie hasn't addressed it. If Armie is, in fact, selling timeshares, I think it's s----y that the media seems to be shaming him for having a 'normal job,'" Brettler said in his email to PEOPLE. He also added in his statement to EW, "What's the story here?"

    Armie Hammer is the great-grandson of oil tycoon Armand Hammer. He was worth an estimated $800 million at the time of his death. But according to sources, Armie "is not on the family payroll."

    Robert Downey Jr. Has Reportedly Allowed Armie Hammer To Stay At His Home

    It has also emerged that actor Robert Downey Jr. has been supporting Armie Hammer after his career hit the skids. He has allegedly allowed the actor to stay at his home, after previously paying for his rehab.

    Variety reported this week that the Hammer has left the Cayman Islands and flown back to Los Angeles to escape public scrutiny.

    A source told the publication that Hammer has been staying at a home owned by the Sherlock Holmes actor, 57, and "attended an A.A. meeting" in the area. The source added that Downey has been providing Hammer with "financial assistance until he gets back on his feet." Hammer is allegedly staying there with estranged wife Elizabeth Chambers, 39, and their two children Harper, seven, and Ford, five.

    The source revealed that it was also Downey who helped encourage the star to enter rehab last year, after his career imploded in January 2021 following sexual assault allegations made against him. The insider confirmed that Downey paid for Hammer to spend close to six months in the Florida rehabilitation facility the Guest House.

    Armie Hammer's Current Net Worth

    Armie Hammer's ex-wife Elizabeth Chambers, 39, filed for divorce in July 2020 following 10 years of marriage. It came after text messages emerged online allegedly showcasing Hammer's violent sexual fantasies. Chambers previously told her Instagram followers in May 2021 that she was "focusing on healing, my babes and work." She added, "A lot I've wanted to share, but hasn't felt right [at the moment]."

    Hammer checked into rehab for spring for drug, alcohol and sex issues. In December 2021, his lawyer said that the star had "left the treatment facility and is doing great." A source also told PEOPLE at the time that Hammer would "continue with outpatient treatments" and that he "takes it very seriously."

"[Armie's] main priority is to be sober and stay sober for the kids and Elizabeth," the insider said at the time. "They're co-parenting effectively, and they're very much intertwined in each other's lives."

    "The past year hasn't been easy, but she and Armie have always had love for each other," the source said. "They're slowly figuring things out as a couple, but being parents to their two kids will always come first."

    As for his current net worth, it doesn't seem as though Hammer is completely broke. Celebrity Net Worth claims his net worth is currently in the six-figure range at $100,000. While other sources indicate that the actor still has a couple of million left.
