    Waa kuma Courtney Cronin?

    Courtney Cronin, oo ah khabiir isboorti oo caan ah oo loogu talagalay Shabakadda Barnaamijyada Madadaalada iyo Ciyaaraha (ESPN), waa qof aad loo ixtiraamo. Waa weriyaha kaliya ee ku guuleystay abaal-marinno badan.

    Immisa jir ayuu Courtney Cronin jiraa?

    Courtney Cronin waxa uu ku dhashay Glenview, Illinois 1dii Agoosto 1990. Courtney Cronin waa 32 jir wakhtigan. Waalidkeed waxay ugu yeeraan Mareykanka Mareykanka. Inkasta oo aan xogta waalidkeed la hayn, haddana way caddahay in ay ku soo barbaartay Maraykanka. Michael waa walaalkeed. Waxbarshadeeda kadib Glenbrook South High School, waxay ku qaadatay shahaadadeeda saxaafada iyo master-keeda ee Isbaanishka Jaamacadda Indiana.

    Courtney Cronin Career - Dulmar
