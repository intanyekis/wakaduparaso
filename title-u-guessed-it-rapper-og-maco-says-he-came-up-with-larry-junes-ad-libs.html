    While haters may accuse OG Maco of being a one-hit-wonder, the rapper confirmed during a recent sit-down with VladTV that he’s been hard at work behind the scenes penning most of today’s biggest rapper tags and ad-libs. He spoke on how the OG Parker tag was his doing and said he even helped Larry June create his TFM motto and all of his adlibs.

    “Larry actually flew me out to California, to The Bay and was like, ‘Bro I’m thinking about quitting this rap shit,'” Maco recalled. “I told him right then I’m like, ‘Bro, you know I got all these crazy ass ad-libs … you need to come up with some shit that’s gonna catch ’em like ’cause you got everything else. You really got the real story, you got the real like you painting a picture, but like they need to feel it just like they hear it.”

    He added, “and I remember that day was when he first did the ‘yee-hee’…and it’s just evolved, and you know now Larry is Larry.”

    Elsewhere in the interview, OG Maco spoke about how Curren$y, who now works closely with Larry June, remains one of his biggest influences to this day and called his Alchemist collab tape Covert Coupe the best mixtape ever.

    “I still think Covert Coupe is like the best mixtape of all time,” he said. “I still feel like as a mixtape, like as a project, I feel like that’s the best mixtape of all time. It’s that and Kush & Orange Juice. Them shits is neck and neck for best mixtape projects of all time.”
