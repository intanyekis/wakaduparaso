    Dorothy Lucey Wiki Biography

    Dorothy Lucey was born on 19 November 1958, in the United States of America, and is a reporter best known for her work in the entertainment sector. She has been involved in the morning news broadcast “Good Day L.A” which was part of the Los Angeles Fox affiliate KTTV. All of her endeavors have helped put her net worth to where it is today.

    How rich is Dorothy Lucey? As of early-2017, sources estimate a net worth that is at $3 million, mostly earned through success in broadcast journalism.     Dorothy attended George Washington University, and would study political science there. She graduated in 1979, and would then try to pursue a career as a reporter, becoming an on-air reporter for the network WNEP which was located in Scranton, Pennsylvania. She then moved to New York City, where she would work as a part of WABC. During her time there, she had a stint alongside Regis Philbin, before moving to join NBC Sports. In the early 1990s, she gained popularity as well as net worth when she became the co-host of the Lifetime Television show “Attitudes”, appearing during the latter part of the show’s run after Jerry Penacoli and Linda Dano left the show. However, the new format was short lived as the new hosts weren’t very well received, and audiences were also not fond of the new tabloid style format.

    After this stint, Dorothy then hosted the show “How’d They Do That” which was aired on CBS. In 1995, she would become part of “Good Day LA” as an entertainment reporter, and was widely involved with the show, as it often aired banter between the three anchors. Instead of the usual news, traffic and weather segments, “Good Day L.A” focused a lot more on feature stories, which included celebrity interviews, food, and fashion segments as well. Lucey stayed with the show for 17 years, increasing her net worth significantly. She covered numerous gossip stories, scandals, and reality shows such as “American Idol”.
