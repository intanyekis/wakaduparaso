    Hollywood is certainly stepping up its game with film releases. Fans of movies are accustomed to seeing one or two big stars in a film and maybe a celebrity cameo. The reasoning behind the choice to keep stars in small numbers primarily has to do with money. The more popular a celebrity, the more they charge for screen time. Ensemble casts used to be a rare commodity, but times are certainly changing.

    Amsterdam, which hit theaters on October 7th of this year, has one of the largest celebrity casts of any movie coming out in 2022. The producers and studios were not cutting corners with this film, and they brought in stars from all realms of the entertainment industry. Ranging from Oscar winners to musicians and comedians, the Amsterdam cast is certainly star-studded. Let’s get into who the stars are of the new film Amsterdam.

    Related: Will Black Panther 2 Be The Biggest Movie Of 2022?

    8 Robert De Niro As General Gil Dillenbeck

    Robert De Niro has been a big name in Hollywood for a long time. He has starred in some of the most popular and critically acclaimed films over the past several decades. De Niro has been nominated for numerous Oscar Awards, winning Best Supporting Actor for his role in The Godfather Part II and Best Actor for Raging Bull.

    In Amsterdam, De Niro plays General Gil Dillenbeck. De Niro has been sparking rumors about finding love yet again. He has been married twice, most recently to Grace Hightower. Fans of the actor are rooting for De Niro to find his special someone.

    7 Rami Malek As Tom Voze

    Robert De Niro is not the only Best Actor Oscar winner in the cast of Amsterdam. Rami Malek joined the cast of the film as Tom Voze. Malek is best known for his Oscar winning performance in the film Bohemian Rhapsody, in which he took on the role of Freddie Mercury. Fans of the film were particularly impressed with his acting during the Live Aid performance scene in the film.

    While now Malek act incredible and Oscar-winning roles, that was not always the case. One of Malek’s first roles in the entertainment industry was a small role as a vampire in the Twilight film franchise. Many Twilight stars have gone on to create impressive careers.

    6 John David Washington As Harold Woodman

    John David Washington is the son of Oscar winner Denzel Washington. He is a former professional athlete and signed as an un-drafted free agent with the St. Louis Rams in 2006. Moving on from football, Washington followed in his father’s footsteps and began his acting journey.

    Washington is known for his starring role in the 2018 film BlacKkKlansman, which also starred Adam Driver. In 2021, Washington shared the screen with Zendaya in the film Malcolm & Marie. He was also the lead in Christopher Nolan’s Tenet. In Amsterdam, Washington plays Harold Woodman.

    5 Margot Robbie As Valerie Voze

    Margot Robbie is a highly successful actress. Her breakout role was in the 2013 film The Wolf of Wall Street, which starred Leonardo DiCaprio. She took on the role of Harley Quinn in the Suicide Squad franchise as well as the role of Kayla Pospisil in the female-empowering film Bombshell. Robbie was nominated for the Oscar award of Best Actress for her work in I, Tonya.

    Fans of Robbie also loved her performance in Quentin Tarantino’s Once Upon a Time in Hollywood. Fans loved seeing her onscreen with Leonardo DiCaprio again. Robbie plays Valerie Voze in Amsterdam.

    4 Chris Rock As Milton King

    Chris Rock is not a typical inclusion in a Hollywood production including Oscar-winners. Rock is a famous comedian, most known for his hilarious stand-up specials. Fans are always shocked to learn that Rock actually has a long list of acting credits. He was in the television show Fargo, the film franchise Grown Ups, and the Madagascar films.

    Rock was the host of this year’s Oscar Awards, which resulted in the infamous “slap.” After making jokes about Jada Smith, Rock was slapped on stage by Will Smith. Everyone was shocked that the actor turned to violence, but Rock carried on hosting despite the interruption.

    3 Anya Taylor-Joy As Libby Voze

    Anya Taylor-Joy has been in the entertainment industry since 2014. She is best known for her lead performance in the critically acclaimed Netflix show The Queen’s Gambit. Fans fell in love with Taylor-Joy’s character in the show. She was nominated for a Primetime Emmy for her work in the beloved television show.

    The actress recently starred in the film Last Night in Soho. She played the young version of a murderer and starred alongside House of the Dragon star Matt Smith. Taylor-Joy also had a role in the hit show Peaky Blinders before taking on Libby Voze in Amsterdam.

    2 Christian Bale As Burt Berendsen

    Christian Bale is one of the most versatile actors in Hollywood. He follows method acting, which is the technique of embodying a character whether or not the cameras are rolling. To follow this technique, Bale has both gained and lost an insane amount of weight for a role.

    In the film Amsterdam, in which Bale plays Burt Berendsen, Bale was told to stop upstaging Taylor Swift during a scene in which they sing together. The director of the film said while shooting, “How about Christian and J.D. [John David Washington] just shut up for this one and let Taylor do it?” Bale knew letting Swift take over was the right decision, saying “we had been drowning out an angel’s voice all day long with our cacophony and our rough, terrible voices.”

    1 Taylor Swift As Liz Meekins

    Taylor Swift is the celebrity cameo everyone was excited about in Amsterdam. Swift is one of the most successful female singers today and has won 11 Grammy Awards over the years. She has specifically won Album of the Year three times for Fearless, 1989, and Folklore. Fans of Swift love how often she changes her sound, showcasing her wide range of musical talent.

    Her role as Liz Meekins in Amsterdam is not the first time Swift has appeared on the big screen. The singer appeared in Cats, The Giver, and Valentine’s Day. She also had a cameo during the season 2 finale of the television show New Girl.
