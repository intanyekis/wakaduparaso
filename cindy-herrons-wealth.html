    Cynthia Ann Herron: A Brief Biography

    Born on September 26, 1961, in San Francisco, California, Cindy Herron is of Swiss and African-American descent. She is a prominent figure in the R&B / Soul / Pop music and acting industry. Herron gained widespread recognition as a member of the group En Vogue, where she has enjoyed a successful career since the 1980s, selling over 20 million albums.

    Her estimated net worth is an impressive $7 million, mainly amassed through her involvement in the music industry.

    Cindy Herron’s Net Worth: $7 Million

    Herron embarked on her career in the entertainment world by winning Miss San Francisco and subsequently Miss California in 1986. She delved into the cabaret scene in San Francisco during the 1980s, making her breakthrough through performances in productions like “Showtune”.

    En Vogue, the R&B / Pop group that Herron is a part of, reached significant milestones in their career, including securing the 2nd position on the Billboard Hot 100 with their single “Hold On” from their debut album “Born To Sing” in 1990. The group’s subsequent albums also garnered critical and commercial success, solidifying their position in the music industry.

    Aside from her musical accomplishments, Herron has also ventured into acting, featuring in various films and TV shows which have contributed to her overall wealth.

    In her personal life, Herron is married to former professional baseball player Glenn Braggs, and they have four children.
