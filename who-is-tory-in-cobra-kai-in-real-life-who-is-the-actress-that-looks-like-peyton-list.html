    Tory Nichols is one of the main characters in Cobra Kai and is portrayed by Peyton List. Dove Cameron has been said to have a similar resemblance to Peyton List.

    Peyton List made her acting debut with the film 27 Dresses and is best known for playing Holly Hills in the Diary of a Wimpy Kid film series.

    Peyton List had a role in the romantic comedy film Something Borrowed in 2011. Peyton List also appeared as Emma Ross on other Disney Channel shows, such as Austin & Ally and I Didn’t Do It.

    Peyton List attended the New Voices School for Academic and Creative Arts (M.S. 443) for middle school, both in New York City.

    Peyton List made an uncredited appearance on the soap opera As the World Turns in 2002. In 2010, Peyton List was cast alongside Robert Pattinson in the film Remember Me as Samantha. The film was a box-office success.

    In 2012, Peyton List appeared in the drama film The Trouble with Cali. In 2016, Peyton List co-starred along with Jacob Bertrand in the Disney Channel Original Movie The Swap, appearing as Ellie O’Brien.
