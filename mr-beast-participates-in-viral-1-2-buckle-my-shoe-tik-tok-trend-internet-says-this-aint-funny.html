    GREENVILLE, NORTH CAROLINA: YouTube king James Stephen Donaldson, widely known as MrBeast, participated in a popular TikTok trend based on the nursery rhyme '1, 2 Buckle My Shoe'.

    TikToker Eddy's '1 2 Buckle My Shoe' soundtrack has gone viral, with other creators incorporating it into their videos. The spoofed nursery rhyme has over 18.1 million views, 4 million likes, and 46,000 comments.

    Also Read: MrBeast suggests Elon Musk to gift a Tesla Cybertruck to YouTuber Marques Brownlee, Internet says 'that would be amazing'

    MrBeast and Eddy collaborate for nursery rhyme trend

    Eddy and MrBeast collaborated to create a video. In the opening of the video, Eddy remarked, introducing MrBeast and talking to the audience, "Yo, come over here. Come over here."

    As the camera frame captured MrBeast, Eddy added, "I'm with MrBeast and we got the brand new." He went on to sing, "1 2 Buckle my shoe; 3 4, buckle some more; 5 6, MrBeast kicks. That is fire!"

    Originally, the trend included shoes, but MrBeast did something different. Instead of wearing shoes, the YouTuber wore black crocs. Fans noticed this and changed the rhyme saying, "One two buckle my mrbeast crocs."

    However, not everyone was impressed by the video. A user commented, "This ain't funny anymore." Someone else wrote, "MrBeast just doing side missions at this point."

    Also Read: Alex wins MrBeast's $450K challenge for enduring 45 days in grocery store, Internet calls it 'most creative ways to give charity'

    In the video, MrBeast wore a full-sleeve, black T-shirt and white shorts, and to complete the trend, he also donned black crocks with a fake yellow buckle on top.

    What is the '1, 2 Buckle My Shoe' trend on TikTok?

    The nursery rhyme '1, 2, Buckle My Shoe' has recently appeared in several TikTok videos. The children's song dates back to the 18th century and has since become a standard in kindergarten classrooms.

    TikTokers from Gen Z have been mocking the song in videos, with the original music taken from a video of a teenager showing off his new buckled shoes.
