    Kim Kardashian Flies to Minnesota to Visit Kris Humphries

    Kim Kardashian is on the move. After landing back on U.S. soil Friday following her abbreviated promotional trip to Australia, the newly single star hopped on a plane again overnight Saturday, this time flying to Minnesota to visit Kris Humphries.

    01 Jun 2024, 00:00

    Kim Kardashian is on the move.

    After landing back on U.S. soil Friday following her abbreviated promotional trip to Australia, the newly single star hopped on a plane again overnight Saturday, this time flying to Minnesota to visit Kris Humphries.

    A source tells Us Weekly Kardashian touched down at Minneapolis-St. Paul Airport Sunday morning. According to TMZ, the 31-year-old flew commercial and traveled with a male companion.

    Humphries, Kardashian's husband of 72 days, has maintained a low profile in his home state in the days since Kardashian filed for divorce October 31.

    "I'm committed to this marriage and everything this covenant represents," a stunned Humphries — who was photographed without his wedddng ring Thursday — told Us. "I love my wife and am devastated to learn she filed for divorce. I'm willing to do whatever it takes to make it work."

    Though the NBA star, 26, says he was blindsided by his new bride's filing, a Kardashian pal tells the new issue of Us the Keeping Up With the Kardashians star saw red flags before their lavish August 20 nuptials.

    "She knew it was wrong before they were even married," a Kardashian insider tells Us. "They were not seeing eye to eye and it felt wrong."
